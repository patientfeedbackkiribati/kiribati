K-PaF Desk Registration Form

This form is used to document and file feedback received from health service users through the K-PaF Office. Each submission should correspond to a single complaint, concern, suggestion, or compliment case. Fields marked * are required.

Section 1 — Intake Information

Details of how the feedback was received.

Section 2 — Concerned Person

Provide the information of the healthcare user (patient) to whom the feedback relates.

Section 3 — Concerned Facility

Select the facility associated with the feedback.

Specify the facility area when available.

Section 4 — Complaint Categories

Please record the feedback as described by the client. Where possible, describe events in chronological order and distinguish between the events themselves and the client's perceptions, feelings, assumptions, or beliefs. Do not include the opinions or interpretations of the person receiving the feedback.

⚠ Note: To ensure patient data security, data entry personnel must not include any personally identifiable information in the complaint summary.

Section 5 — Stage of Patient Journey

Please select the stage of the patient's healthcare journey to which the feedback relates. This helps identify where in the care process the experience, concern, or issue occurred.

Section 6 — Requested Follow-up

Please indicate what the client expects from K-PaF and/or MHMS in response to this feedback.
